Microsoft Strategic Shift 2025–2026: The entire certification portfolio has been realigned around AI Agents · Microsoft Foundry · Copilot Studio · RAG · MCP · A2A. Certifications (AI-102, AZ-204, DP-100, AI-900, PL-200, PL-500, PL-600, MB-280, MB-700, AZ-500) are being retired and replaced by new credentials designed for the agentic AI era.
